About

I've been dancing since the end of 2009. When I came across swing dancing, I fell in love. My partners are Scott Angelius (Knoxville, TN), Blake Thiessen (Los Angeles, CA), Joshua McLean (Seattle, WA).

Grace has been dancing, competing and teaching for years. As a competitor she has placed and won in regional, national and international contest. She loves seeing people grow as they learn this dance. Focusing on connection, Grace teaches techniques that help dancers be versatile and creative so they can dance with anyone.
